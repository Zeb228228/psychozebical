How to add a video from YouTube to your website in 5 simple steps.

Anatoly Ulitin

2025

Getting videos to your website from YouTube is blah blah blah very important nowadays because blah blah more intro text blah blah.

1. Open YouTube.

Via the URL or whatever, just do it.

2. Find the desired video and open it.

You can use the searchbar and then just click on the video.

3. Click "share" below the video.

4. In the pop out, click on "Embed".

5. Click on "copy" in the bottom right corner and paste anywhere into your HTML!

Note that by changing the "width" and "height" parameters in the beginning of the line of code you just pasted you can change the size of the video!

Enjoy!!!
