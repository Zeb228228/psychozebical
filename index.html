An apartment with 70 cats was found in Krasnoe Selo


-Anatoly Ulitin, Daily News, 29th April 2025


A pensioner picked up the little cats from the street, fed them and watered them. But no one canceled animal instincts, and the cats began to reproduce at a furious rate. The woman tried to find good homes for the kittens, but due to the number of them, it was not possible to do this quickly.

Here's a quote from the local agency, Dobrodog:

Recently, the pensioner had to go to the hospital for a long time, so the crowd of whiskered ones was left without an owner. Volunteers from the Dobrodog shelter came to the woman's aid and are now actively solving the issue of finding new moms and dads for the fluffy ones
